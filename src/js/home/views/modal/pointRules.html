Le jeux des pronosctics

What are the rules?

You cannot win or lose anything here. This is only for fun. You may only make bets for a whole match, not only for a half. You can make bets for every match that is not started already. No one, except you, will see your bet until the match starts. You get points if you made a bet for a match and the match is over. You win the game if you are rank 1 on the highscore list after the final match.

How do I score?

You get for a correct bet OR You get for the correct goal difference OR You get for the correct winner OR You get zero points in any other case or if you did not make a bet

Legal rules

You may not change your name to anything offensive. The game master may exclude you from the game at any time. Recourse to the courts is not permitted.
